# Example: Feature-to-emotional-value transformation

## Input

Product: lightweight waterproof commuter backpack

Specs:

- Waterproof fabric
- 16-inch laptop compartment
- Foldable side pocket
- 22L capacity

Audience: office commuters and short business travelers.

## Expected output shape

- Functional value: protects work items from rain and reduces bag-switching.
- Scenario: rainy commute, airport security, one-day business trip.
- Pain point: arriving wet, disorganized, or looking unprepared.
- Emotional value: calm, prepared, in control.
- Copy angle: "Stay composed when the weather is not. Your laptop, documents, and daily essentials stay protected in one clean commuter bag."

## Risky claims to avoid

- Guaranteed waterproof under all conditions.
- Fake reviews.
- Unsupported durability claims.
